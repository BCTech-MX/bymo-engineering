Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 23, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how Black Cube Technologies SRL de CV ("Bymo", "we", "us", or "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website and our applications (collectively, the "Services"), including any application that uses Meta (Facebook/Instagram) Login. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described in this policy.

1. Who We Are

Bymo is a brand operated by Black Cube Technologies SRL de CV, a boutique software engineering firm based in Mexico City, Mexico, serving clients across the Americas and Europe. For any privacy-related questions, you can reach us at contacto@bymo.com.mx.

2. Information We Collect

We only collect the information needed to provide and improve our Services. Depending on how you interact with us, this may include:

Information you provide directly

  • Contact details — name, email address, company, and any message you submit through our contact form.
  • Account information — when you sign in to an application using Meta Login, we may receive your name, email address, profile picture, and Meta user ID, subject to the permissions you grant.

Information collected automatically

  • Usage and device data — IP address, browser type, device type, pages visited, and timestamps, collected to keep the Services secure and reliable.
  • Cookies and similar technologies — used to maintain sessions and understand basic usage. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

Information from Meta (Facebook / Instagram) Login

If you choose to log in using Meta, we receive only the data you explicitly authorize during the permission flow — typically your public profile and email address. We do not receive your Meta password, and we never post to your account without your consent.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Provide, operate, and maintain our Services.
  • Authenticate you and manage your account.
  • Respond to your inquiries and provide support.
  • Improve, personalize, and secure the Services.
  • Comply with legal obligations and prevent fraud or abuse.

We do not sell your personal information, and we do not use it for advertising profiling.

4. How We Share Your Information

We share personal information only in limited circumstances:

  • Service providers — trusted vendors who help us operate the Services (for example, hosting and form-processing providers), bound by confidentiality obligations.
  • Legal requirements — when required by law, regulation, legal process, or enforceable governmental request.
  • Protection of rights — to protect the rights, property, or safety of Bymo, our users, or the public.

5. Data Retention

We retain personal information only as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes described in this policy, to comply with legal obligations, resolve disputes, and enforce our agreements. When data is no longer needed, we delete or anonymize it.

6. Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures — including encryption in transit and access controls — to protect your information against unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. No method of transmission or storage is completely secure, but we work to safeguard your data.

7. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the right to access, correct, delete, or restrict the processing of your personal data, and to withdraw consent at any time. To exercise these rights, contact us at contacto@bymo.com.mx.

Deleting your data and Meta account disconnection

If you signed in with Meta and would like to disconnect the application or delete the data associated with your account, you can:

  • Remove the app from your Meta account settings under Settings & Privacy → Apps and Websites; or
  • Email us at contacto@bymo.com.mx with the subject "Data Deletion Request", and we will delete your personal data within 30 days.

8. Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to children under the age of 13 (or the minimum age required in your jurisdiction). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can remove it.

9. International Data Transfers

Bymo operates from Mexico and serves clients internationally. Your information may be processed in countries other than your own. Where required, we take steps to ensure your data receives an adequate level of protection.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the "Last updated" date at the top of this page. Material changes will be communicated through the Services where appropriate.
